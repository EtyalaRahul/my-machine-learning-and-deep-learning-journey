Mahendra Singh Dhoni, commonly known as MS Dhoni,
is a former Indian international cricketer and captain.
He led India to three ICC trophies:
- ICC T20 World Cup 2007
- ICC Cricket World Cup 2011
- ICC Champions Trophy 2013

Dhoni is known for his calm attitude,
sharp decision making, and finishing ability.
He also served as a wicket-keeper batsman
and is regarded as one of the greatest captains
in the history of the sport.
